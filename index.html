Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the support period?

During the support period, I'll fix any bugs, help with minor adjustments, and ensure your website continues to work smoothly. Major changes or new features would be quoted separately.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes! If your needs don't perfectly fit any of these packages, I'd be happy to create a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements.

How do revisions work?

After I present the initial design, you can request changes. Each set of changes counts as one revision. I'll continue refining until you're completely satisfied, within the revision limit of your package.

What about hosting and domain?

Hosting and domain registration are not included in these packages, but I can help you set them up. I recommend reliable providers based on your needs and budget.

How do we get started?

Simply click the "Get Started" button on your preferred package, and I'll contact you within 24 hours to discuss your project in detail and begin the process.
